Traveling to France

To travel to France, ensure your passport is valid for 3+ months past your trip, check if you need a visa (many don't for short stays), and prepare for the new EU ETIAS authorization in 2025. Plan for the best seasons (spring/fall), budget for costs (~$2k/week), book transport like the TGV train, and be mindful of safety by securing belongings and watching for petty theft.


Before You Go

Passport:
Valid for at least 3 months after your departure from the Schengen area, with blank pages.
Visa/ETIAS:
U.S. citizens don't need a visa for more than 90 days, but must register for ETIAS (Electronic Travel Authorization) starting in 2025. Check the official France-Visas site for your specific requirements.
Currency:
Declare amounts over €10,000.
